How it works...

To filter by concentration categories:

Click on the categories at the top of the graph.


To show all the concentrations again:

Press the refresh button next to the categories.


To change the year shown:

Use the slider on top.


To see line graphs for each concentration:

Mouse over a bar, and the line graph for that concentration will appear.

Stories

Click on the CS or EC or BIO story books on the left to see the stories of those concentrations.
