Piazza courses to LinkedIn profile

Instructions

Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar to use it:

Piazza to LinkedIn


Make sure you are logged in to LinkedIn first.

Log in to piazza.com and click the bookmarklet.

You should be redirected to linkedin.com/profile/edit.
Click the bookmarklet.

You should now see the courses list populated with your courses from Piazza!
